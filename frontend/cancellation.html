Cancellation Policy

At GymGarage, we strive to provide the best products and customer experience. However, we understand that sometimes plans change. Please review our cancellation policy below:

  • Order Cancellation: You may cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it, provided that the item has not yet been shipped. Once the order has been shipped, cancellations will not be possible. To cancel an order, please contact our customer support team with your order details.
  • Non-Refundable Orders: Certain items such as custom or made-to-order products are non-cancellable once the order has been processed.
  • Refund Processing for Cancellations: If your order is successfully canceled, your refund will be processed back to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

If you would like to cancel an order that you have placed on the website, kindly send us an email as soon as possible at gymgarage@gmail.com, with your order number as the subject, to avoid complications.

Orders that have already been shipped out may still be canceled. However, please note that certain fees may be deducted from the total refund amount:

  • 5% of the total payment â€“ if paid through a credit card.
  • For large exercise machines paid via bank deposit, a 5% processing fee will be deducted from the total refund.
  • Customers are responsible for covering the cost of returning purchased items.

Once the cancellation is successful, a confirmation email will be sent to your registered email address. Please note that canceled orders cannot be reactivated. If you change your mind, a new order must be placed through the usual checkout process.

Automatic cancellations may occur in the following situations:
  • Suspected fraudulent transactions.
  • Incomplete customer details (e.g. address, contact details) causing failed deliveries.
  • Orders with a maximum of two (2) failed delivery attempts.
  • Unpaid orders via bank deposit that are outstanding for over 48 hours.
  • Orders not covered by our serviceable area.

For further inquiries, feel free to reach out to us at gymgarage@gmail.com.
